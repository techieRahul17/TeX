# TeX App - User Manual

Welcome to **TeX**, your secure and customizable chatting platform! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started with the app.

*Note: The current beta application is approximately 79-80 MB. We are actively working on optimizations to reduce the app size in future updates.*

---

## 1. Installation & Setup
1. **Download the APK:** You will receive the TeX app as an APK file for testing.
2. **Install:** Tap on the APK file to begin installation. If prompted, allow your device to install apps from your current source, and complete the installation/update.
3. **Launch:** Once the installation is done, locate the **TeX** app in your phone's app list and open it.

## 2. Login & Registration
To keep your chats secure and synced, you need to create an account:
- **Email Registration:** Register with your email address. You will be sent a verification link/code. Verify your email to complete the setup.
- **Google Login:** For a faster experience, tap the Google Login button to instantly log in using your Google account.

## 3. Profile Setup
Before you start chatting, let's set up your identity:
1. Navigate to the **Profile** section of the app.
2. Enter and set up your unique **Username**. 
3. Tap **Save**. *(Your friends will use this username to find you!)*

## 4. App Themes & Global Customization
Make the app yours! 
- Explore the settings to change your **App Theme**. 
- Play around with the available colors and modes to find the perfect look for your overall app interface.

## 5. Private Chatting & Adding Friends
TeX offers secure, encrypted chatting. Here is how to connect with someone:
1. Navigate back to the main **Chats** page.
2. Tap the **Add Friends** icon located at the top of the screen.
3. Search for your friend using their specific **Username**.
4. Send them a request.
5. Once your friend **accepts** your request, a chat room will be opened for you both.
6. Start chatting! All your 1-on-1 messages are sent in a fully **encrypted way**.
7. **Custom Chat Themes:** Want a different vibe for specific friends? You can beautifully customize individual chat themes right from within your opened chat!

## 6. Group Chats
Want to talk to multiple friends at once? Create a group!
1. On the main Chats page, tap the **+ (Plus)** icon in the bottom right corner.
2. Create a new group and add your friends. 
3. This will send them an **acceptance request** to join the group.
4. Once they have successfully joined, you (as the creator) can manage the group. You have the power to make them **Co-Admins** (or remove their admin rights).
5. Enjoy seamless group conversations with all your friends!
